Jin Wu（吴荩/吳藎）


Jin Wu, Bachelor in Applied Science

I am Jin Wu from China. I was born in May, 1994, in Zhenjiang, Jiangsu, China. I started C++ programming since 2004, the MFC programming in 2006, and the Qt programming in 2009. I obtained my Bachelor's degree from University of Electronic Science and Technolology of China (UESTC, 電子科技大學), supervised by Prof. Zebo Zhou and Prof. Rui Li, co-supervised by Prof. Bin Gao and Prof. Yuhua Cheng. In 2013, I was a visiting student with Groep T, Katholieke Universiteit Leuven (KU Leuven). I worked as a shareholder of several companies of unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) services since 2012. Previously, I worked as an engineer in Tencent Robotics X (騰訊機器人), under the administration of Dr. Zhengyou Zhang. I worked as an active educator of open-source UAV systems like PX4 and ArduPilot on embedded programming and optimization in China from 2015 to 2018. Till now, my related research works have been reported in over 100 journal and conference papers, which include top-tier publications, such as IEEE Transactions on Robotics, IEEE Transactions on Cybernetics, IEEE Transactions on Aerospace and Electronic Systems, AIAA Journal of Guidance Control and Dynamics. Currently, I serve as a PhD student in the Robotics and Multi-Perception Lab (RAM-LAB), Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST, 香港科技大學), Hong Kong SAR, China, under the supervision of Prof. Ming Liu（劉明 教授）. Currently, I am also an engineer with Unity Drive Inc. (一清創新), Shenzhen, China. We are working toward the best autonomous driving company all over the world!
