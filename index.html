Jin Wu


Jin Wu, Bachelor in Applied Science

I am Jin Wu from China. I was born in May, 1994, in Zhenjiang, Jiangsu, China. I started C++ programming since 2004, the MFC programming in 2006, and the Qt programming in 2009. I obtained my Bachelor's degree from University of Electronic Science and Technolology of China (UESTC), supervised by Prof. Zebo Zhou and Prof. Rui Li, co-supervised by Prof. Bin Gao and Prof. Yuhua Cheng. I worked as a shareholder of several companies of unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) services since 2012. Previously, I worked as an engineer in Tencent Robotics X, under the administration of Dr. Zhengyou Zhang. I worked as an active educator of open-source UAV systems like PX4 and ArduPilot on embedded programming and optimization in China from 2015 to 2018. Currently, I serve as a PhD student in the Robotics and Multi-Perception Lab (RAM-LAB), Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), Hong Kong SAR, China, under the supervision of Prof. Ming Liu. Since 2014, I have collaborated closely with Prof. Hassen Fourati from GIPSA Lab, University of Grenoble Alpes, Prof. Jinling Wang from New South Wales University (NSWU), Prof. Federico Thomas from Institut de Robòtica i Informàtica Industrial, Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya, Prof. Yuanxin Wu from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), Prof. Changbin Yu from Australian National University (ANU), Prof. Yulong Huang from Harbin Engineering University (HEU), Dr. Yu Zheng from Tencent Robotics X and Prof. Wei He from University of Science and Technology Beijing.

Recently, I work closely with Dr. Chengxi Zhang from Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, China; Dr. Yi Jiang from City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong SAR, China; and Prof. Chong Li from Ocean University of China, Qingdao, China.
