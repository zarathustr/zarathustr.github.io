Jin Wu（吴荩/吳藎）


Jin Wu, Bachelor in Applied Science

I am Jin Wu from China. I was born in May, 1994, in Zhenjiang, Jiangsu, China. I started C++ programming since 2004, the MFC programming in 2006, and the Qt programming in 2009. I obtained my Bachelor's degree from University of Electronic Science and Technolology of China (UESTC), supervised by Prof. Zebo Zhou and Prof. Rui Li, co-supervised by Prof. Bin Gao and Prof. Yuhua Cheng. In 2013, I was a visiting student with Groep T, Katholieke Universiteit Leuven (KU Leuven). I worked as a shareholder of several companies of unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) services since 2012. Previously, I worked as an engineer in Tencent Robotics X, under the administration of Dr. Zhengyou Zhang. I worked as an active educator of open-source UAV systems like PX4 and ArduPilot on embedded programming and optimization in China from 2015 to 2018. Currently, I serve as a PhD student in the Robotics and Multi-Perception Lab (RAM-LAB), Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), Hong Kong SAR, China, under the supervision of Prof. Ming Liu（劉明 教授）. Currently, I am also an engineer with Unity Drive Inc., Shenzhen, China. We are working toward the best autonomous driving company all over the world!
