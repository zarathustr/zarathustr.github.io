Jin Wu

Jin Wu, Bachelor in Applied Science

I am Jin Wu from China. I started C++ programming since 2004. I obtained my Bachelor's degree from University of Electronic Science and Technolology of China (UESTC), supervised by Prof. Zebo Zhou and Prof. Rui Li, co-supervised by Prof. Bin Gao and Prof. Yuhua Cheng. I worked as a shareholder of several companies of UAV services since 2012. Previously, I worked as an engineer in Tencent Robotics X, under the administration of Dr. Zhengyou Zhang. Currently, I am working as a navigation algorithm engineer in Unity Drive Inc., Shenzhen, China. I also serve as a PhD student in the Robotics and Multi-Perception Lab (RAM-LAB), Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), Hong Kong SAR, China. Since 2014, I have collaborated closely with Prof. Hassen Fourati from GIPSA Lab, University of Grenoble Alpes, Prof. Jinling Wang from New South Wales University, Prof. Federico Thomas from Institut de Robòtica i Informàtica Industrial, Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya.
